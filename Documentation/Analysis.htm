The base of my design is split into 2 sections. The first section is the managerial and business side. This will have a separate login section to the over side. The managerial section consists of multiple concepts the main function will be a tournament organization. This will be a simple document which will have several players inputted into it, their team and their handicap. It will then work out the seeding and create a graph of the tournament and all the rounds within that tournament. To help with the design of the tournament Mr. Gabriel will walk me through how it works so the algorithm can be created and be just like the normal paper versions that currently exist.  The tournament organizer or team managers will then be able to watch the players as they go around the course seeing what results have been inputted into each hole, and maybe see their gps on a map of the course but that would be very advanced and is just an idea. Other functions possible for the manager are score keeping so that past results from matches can be seen or even just past training matches. This can show improvement of the player or a hole in which they struggle on. In addition, an idea would be for the manager or coach to have a lesson section where anyone could book a session with him and see when he is available to play and learn.

 

The other section of this program is for the players. This will be a directory of every player belonging to the application. Each player will have their team, Handicap and age group as part of there data. Within the player section you will be able to start a match which will load a view of the course with all the distances and terrain so you can see the angle of the slope of the green or the distance from tee to hole so you can choose which club would be best. The application will try and record as much data as possible to help improve your shot like the distance from hole before the shot and after the shot shown as a percentage. The accuracy of your shot. To help with the valuable information Mr. Hancocks, one of my clients will tell me which information is worth saving. The information will be kept private although every player in the tournament will be allowed to see you distance from the hole. Finally, each player can have a list of friends and see who is currently playing where and how they are doing.

The main aim is just to make tournaments and matches easier to organize and help improve the average players game. The application can be used whenever so doesn’t need to be need to be a school training session and the manger can see who’s been practicing when may be through an alert on their phone saying, “This player has started playing at …”. I don’t want the course to just be Stowe’s the main aim is to have a large amount to course on to the system and you to be able to download multiple maps so when u go to a new course u can immediately download the course and start using a gigantic amount of data.
